Recent Work

Place of Destination

Place of Destination is a 2D PixelArt Action Roguelike game where you have to survive from the monsters each round and collect money from killing them to upgrade yourself with more powerful items. In this project, I led a team of 4 members.

Award

The project made its way into GAME TALENT SHOWCASE 2022 Presented by Bitkub where only thirty teams were selected.

BonkSlayer

BonkSlayer is a 2D Top-Down Shooter that lets the player play as a Shiba dog based on the internet meme “bonk”. The player has to fight with other dogs using a baseball bat and survive as long as possible. Not only this is the first game I have ever created it was also my first time leading a team of 4 people.

LifeForceTenka Clone

LifeForceTenka Clone is an FPS game that lets the players play through the levels and overcome various obstacles within the game such as drones and this game was made entirely in Unreal Engine 4 using Blueprint. Since this is a solo project, I have followed the whole course and most of the assets are obtained from the Unreal Engine 4 FPS:Lifeforce Tenka Clone course.
